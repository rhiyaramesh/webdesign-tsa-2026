Welcome to Virginia Beach

Virginia Beach is more than a popular beach destination. It is a city shaped by culture, historic places, landmarks, and local legends that continue to capture people’s imagination.

Location

Located on the southeastern coast of Virginia along the Atlantic Ocean.

Known For

Boardwalk views, coastal tourism, history, and local folklore.

Grace Sherwood

Grace Sherwood was known as the “Witch of Pungo.” She became one of the most famous legends in Virginia Beach history.

King Neptune

Did You Know?

King Neptune

The King Neptune statue is over 34 feet tall and weighs more than 12 tons, making it one of the most recognizable landmarks in Virginia Beach.

Blackbeard

Did You Know?

Blackbeard

Blackbeard was one of the most feared pirates of the early 1700s, and his story is still connected to coastal legends in Virginia.
