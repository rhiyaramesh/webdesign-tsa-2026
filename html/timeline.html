Timeline

Look into the development of Virginia Beach.

1607

Cape Henry Landing

English settlers from the Jamestown settlement first landed at Cape Henry before moving inland. This marks the beginning of English influence in the region.

1888

Birth of a Beach Destination

A railroad line and hotel were built, making Virginia Beach a popular vacation spot. Tourism began shaping the city’s identity.

1906

The Iconic Boardwalk

The first wooden boardwalk was built, attracting more visitors and businesses. It later evolved into today’s modern oceanfront boardwalk.

1923

Official Town Status

Virginia Beach officially became an incorporated town, growing steadily as a resort community.

1963

Modern City Created

Virginia Beach merged with Princess Anne County, forming the large independent city we know today.

2007

Historic Preventation

The Cape Henry Lighthouse gained increased recognition as one of the oldest lighthouses in the U.S., symbolizing the city’s history.

2019

Virginia Beach Tragedy

A mass shooting at a municipal building deeply impacted the city. The community came together to honor victims and promote unity.
