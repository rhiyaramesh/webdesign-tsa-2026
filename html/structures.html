Cape Henry Lighthouse

The Cape Henry Lighthouse was the first public building funded by the newly formed US government in 1789, and was authorized by George Washington and overseen by Alexander Hamilton. The Lighthouse is situated near the “First Landing” site, where English settlers first set foot on their way to settle in Jamestown, and has guided sea travelers to safety for almost 100 years.

Grand Cavalier Hotel

The Grand Cavalier Hotel, Virginia Beach's premier luxury resort, has a rich history dating back nearly a century. During World War II, it was used by the U.S. Navy as a radar training center and has hosted ten U.S. presidents, including Calvin Coolidge and John F. Kennedy, along with icons like Elizabeth Taylor and Muhammad Ali.
